Features

Rock Band 2 Deluxe enhances the look of vanilla Rock Band 2 and adds much-desired quality-of-life upgrades, on stage and off it. And yes, it's for PS3 and Xbox 360, so you can play as you like. Here's some of what's on offer:

Gameplay upgrades

  • No strum limit
  • Static fills (play the drum chart exactly as intended!)
  • No flames modifier for super hyper accuracy play
  • Double bass support on stock drum kits
  • Autoplay modifier for easy chart demonstrations
  • Gamepad play modifier for our Brazilian friends

Visual overhauls

  • Improved tracks with detailed gems and better smashers
  • 1080p support (emulator recommended)
  • 60fps venues
  • Black venue/focus mode modifier for when you need them frames
  • "Fireworks" gems modifier :tim:

Restored and ported content

These require Rock Band 1 and Lego Rock Band exports to work

  • Rock Band unexportables included with Rock Band 2 Deluxe
  • All genres from Lego Rock Band, Rock Band 3, Rock Band 4
  • Menu music from every single main Rock Band title
  • NPC characters playable

Fit and finish

  • Set Gem Colors menu (GRYBO? ROYGB? ROYOR? GGGGG? You got it :EZ:)
  • Song title always on and detailed song title modifiers
  • Chart authors listed at song start
  • Expert default difficulty (no more selecting Medium on accident!)
  • No more lost fans over quitting and restarting
